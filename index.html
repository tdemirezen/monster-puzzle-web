Privacy Policy - Monster Puzzle

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Introduction

SycamoreGames ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Monster Puzzle mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

Google Play Games Services: When you sign in with Google Play Games, we access your public profile information (display name and profile picture) to display on leaderboards. We do not store this information on our servers.

Game Progress: Your game progress (level, stars, coins) is stored locally on your device. Leaderboard scores are submitted to Google Play Games Services.

Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the game. This includes anonymous usage data such as session duration and feature usage. No personally identifiable information is collected.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain leaderboard functionality
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To improve the game experience through analytics

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics

Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device. Clearing the app data or uninstalling the game will remove all local data. Leaderboard scores stored on Google Play Games Services are subject to Google's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
